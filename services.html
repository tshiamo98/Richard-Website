Our Services

Comprehensive electrical engineering consulting services for building services and PV solar systems. We provide independent engineering design, analysis, and project guidance.

Electrical Masterplanning

What it is

Strategic planning of electrical infrastructure for buildings, campuses, or developments. This includes load forecasting, utility coordination, substation planning, and distribution strategy.

Why it matters

Proper masterplanning ensures electrical systems can accommodate future growth, identifies infrastructure requirements early, and avoids costly changes during construction.

Typical applications

  • New commercial or residential developments
  • Campus expansions for schools or universities
  • Industrial park infrastructure planning
  • Mixed-use development electrical strategy

Reticulation and Distribution

What it is

Design of electrical distribution systems from the point of supply to final circuits. Includes transformer sizing, cable routing, protective device coordination, and distribution board design.

Why it matters

Efficient distribution minimizes energy losses, ensures voltage stability, and provides reliable power to all areas of a building or facility. Proper protection coordination prevents equipment damage.

Typical applications

  • Main distribution board (MDB) and sub-distribution design
  • Cable sizing and routing for new buildings
  • Electrical upgrades for existing facilities
  • Voltage drop calculations for long cable runs

Lighting Design

What it is

Technical design of lighting systems including luminaire selection, lighting control design, emergency lighting, and daylight integration. Includes illumination calculations and energy efficiency analysis.

Why it matters

Proper lighting design affects occupant comfort, safety, and productivity. Energy-efficient lighting reduces operational costs and meets environmental standards.

Typical applications

  • Commercial office lighting with controls
  • Educational facility classroom lighting
  • Industrial area and warehouse lighting
  • Exterior and security lighting design

Small Power and Distribution Boards

What it is

Design of socket outlet circuits, appliance connections, and final distribution boards. Includes circuit protection, earthing, and isolation design for user safety.

Why it matters

Proper small power design ensures adequate outlets for equipment, prevents circuit overloads, and provides safe electrical connections for building occupants.

Typical applications

  • Office workstation power requirements
  • Laboratory and workshop power design
  • Kitchen and appliance circuit design
  • IT equipment and server room power

Data Centres

What it is

Specialized electrical design for data centres including redundant power supplies, uninterruptible power systems (UPS), generator backup, and precision cooling power requirements.

Why it matters

Data centres require extremely high reliability and redundancy. Proper electrical design prevents downtime, protects sensitive equipment, and ensures continuous operation.

Typical applications

  • Enterprise data centre design
  • Co-location facility electrical infrastructure
  • Server room upgrades and expansions
  • Edge computing facility design

Backup Power Solutions

What it is

Design of backup power systems including generators, UPS systems, battery storage, and automatic transfer switches. Includes load analysis, fuel storage, and compliance with municipal requirements.

Why it matters

Backup power ensures critical operations continue during utility outages. Proper design considers runtime requirements, load prioritization, and seamless transition between power sources.

Typical applications

  • Hospital and healthcare facility backup
  • Data centre UPS and generator systems
  • Industrial process continuity planning
  • Commercial building essential services backup

PV Solar Systems

What it is

Complete photovoltaic system design including solar array sizing, inverter selection, battery storage integration, grid connection, and monitoring systems. Compliance with municipal and utility requirements.

Why it matters

PV solar reduces electricity costs, provides energy independence, and supports sustainability goals. Professional engineering ensures system safety, performance, and regulatory compliance.

Typical applications

  • Residential rooftop solar installations
  • Commercial and industrial solar systems
  • Agricultural solar for irrigation and operations
  • Solar plants and power purchase agreements (PPAs)
  • Grid-tied and hybrid solar systems
