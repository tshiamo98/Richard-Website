Project Experience

Our electrical engineering experience spans multiple sectors and project types. Below are representative examples of project categories we have worked on.

Project details are generalized to protect client confidentiality while demonstrating our experience.

Educational and Public Buildings

Project Type

School buildings, university facilities, libraries, municipal buildings, healthcare facilities

Electrical Role

Full electrical design and consulting services from concept to construction completion

Scope Included

  • Electrical masterplanning for campus expansions
  • Lighting design for classrooms and specialized spaces
  • Power distribution for IT labs and science facilities
  • Emergency lighting and backup power for critical areas
  • Compliance with educational facility regulations

Commercial and Office Buildings

Project Type

Office towers, retail centers, mixed-use developments, hotels, conference facilities

Electrical Role

Electrical systems design, tenant power distribution, lighting design, and energy efficiency analysis

Scope Included

  • Main distribution and sub-distribution board design
  • Energy-efficient lighting with control systems
  • Small power design for workstations and equipment
  • Generator and UPS systems for critical loads
  • Coordination with architects and other consultants

Industrial and Infrastructure Projects

Project Type

Manufacturing plants, warehouses, processing facilities, water treatment plants, pump stations

Electrical Role

Industrial electrical design, motor control centers, process power distribution, and hazardous area classification

Scope Included

  • Motor control center (MCC) design and specification
  • Process equipment power and control design
  • Industrial lighting for production areas
  • Power factor correction and energy monitoring
  • Compliance with industrial safety standards

Backup Power Systems

Project Type

Generator installations, UPS systems, battery storage, automatic transfer systems

Electrical Role

Backup power system design, load analysis, generator sizing, and integration with existing electrical systems

Scope Included

  • Critical load analysis and prioritization
  • Generator sizing and fuel system design
  • UPS and battery system design for IT loads
  • Automatic transfer switch (ATS) coordination
  • Compliance with municipal generator regulations

PV Solar and Renewable Energy

Project Type

Residential solar, commercial solar installations, agricultural solar, solar plants, grid-tied systems

Electrical Role

Complete PV system design, yield analysis, grid connection applications, and system commissioning

Scope Included

  • Solar resource assessment and system sizing
  • Inverter selection and DC/AC system design
  • Grid connection applications and compliance
  • Battery storage integration design
  • Monitoring system design and performance analysis
