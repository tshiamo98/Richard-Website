About Electrical Engineering Consulting

Professional electrical engineering consulting focused on building services and PV solar systems across South Africa.

Our Company Information

Our Focus

We provide independent electrical engineering consulting services for building services and photovoltaic solar systems. Our practice focuses on the technical design and analysis required for safe, compliant, and practical electrical installations.

As consulting engineers, we work with developers, architects, and building owners to design electrical systems that meet functional requirements while considering installation, operation, and maintenance realities.

Our Approach

Electrical engineering design requires balancing technical requirements, regulatory compliance, and practical constraints. Our approach is based on engineering principles rather than standard specifications, resulting in solutions tailored to each project.

Compliance

Designs comply with SANS standards, municipal regulations, and utility requirements. We stay current with regulatory changes and industry best practices.

Coordination

Electrical systems must coordinate with architectural, structural, and mechanical elements. We work collaboratively with other consultants to integrate electrical design effectively.

Practicality

Engineering solutions must be installable, maintainable, and operable. We consider construction methods, material availability, and long-term operation in our designs.

Our Principles
